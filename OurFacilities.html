Our Facilities

Indoor Courts

Our state-of-the-art indoor courts are equipped to handle all weather conditions, providing an ideal environment for training and competitive play throughout the year.

Outdoor Courts

Enjoy our spacious outdoor courts, perfect for those who prefer training in fresh air with professional-grade surfaces for various sports, including basketball, tennis, and more.

Fitness Center

Our modern fitness center is fully equipped with the latest machines and weights, providing a complete environment for strength and cardio training under expert guidance.

Swimming Pool

Our Olympic-sized swimming pool is perfect for both competitive training and recreational swimming, with top-notch safety measures in place for all ages.

Yoga Studio

Experience peace and flexibility training in our yoga studio, a calm space designed for mindfulness sessions and stretching routines.
